    OUR SERVICES


    SECURITY GUARDS

    Physical Guarding (deployment of unarmed and uniformed security guards) is our most frequently offered security service across client types and locations. Our security guards are carefully recruited after thorough background and police verification and are rigorously trained in their duties before being deployed at a customer site. Professionally trained men and women security guards perform a range of security services that are tailored to the customer’s business and needs, and these include:

  • Protect the client’s assets and property from unauthorised access, vandalism or theft
  • Monitor entrances and exits; acts to prevent unapproved or unlawful entry; movement of people and vehicles, and parking.
  • Patrol the premises regularly to check for irregularities and to inspect protection devices and fire control equipment
  • Perform front office duties such as visitor entry, maintaining registers and other documentation as required by the customer
  • Respond to incidents of fire, medical emergency, bomb threat, flooding, water discharge, elevator emergency, and other incidents.

    • ARMED GUARDS

    We provide Armed Security Guards to customers who require high security coverage, such as Financial Institutions, ATM’s, Jewelers and so on. These security guards hold government licensed firearms and are rigorously trained to respond to unusual or emergency situations at the customer’s site using the appropriate escalation of force level / armed response as per established protocol.

    ESCORT GUARDS

    There are certain circumstances and situations that necessitate having a uniformed security guard escort you, your employees or your customers from one location to another. This is especially relevant to organisations looking to extend additional protection to their employees who work night shifts or odd hours. We have seen by experience that the presence of escort guards dramatically reduces the chances of you or your people from becoming needless victims of crime or accidents. At Stalwart, we have a separate escort services vertical that caters to this type of specialised requirements. Recruiting escort security guards involves a rigorous, multi-point background check as well as an exhaustive training in areas such as first aid and emergency rescue. The types of clients that typically require employee escort services include BPO’s, IT Companies, Industries, Hospitals, Check Cashing Businesses and so on.

    EVENT MANAGEMENT SECURITY

    Stalwart provides part-time security guards on day hire basis to guard concerts, exhibitions and other public gatherings. Our services in this area include uniformed guarding, crowd and parking control, access control and coordination with local law enforcement personnel. We typically need an advance notice of one month to organize this type of security for large events.
